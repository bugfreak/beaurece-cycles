Hello.

If you want to do a nice tour on your Mountainbike around Großalmerode and need a little help where to go to, or to learn basic MTB-Skills to ride safer or easier, I can give you a hand.
If your Kid want´s to go for a fun Mountainbike tours with other kids and lear how to ride safely and environmental friendly, come and join our Kids-MTB program.
As a local Mountainbiker since the early 90´s we´ll find some sweet spots for you and/or your kid to have some fun hours with the bike in beautiful nature.

Summer activities

Every second Saturday at 10:00 we meet for our Kids-MTB program near the Tenniscourts of Großalmerode for ca. 1,5hrs.

On the last Sunday of the Month we meet near the Tenniscourts of Großalmerode with a bunch of grown ups to have fun on a group ride. We will be on tour for about 2-3hrs.
